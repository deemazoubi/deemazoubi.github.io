About Me

My Story

Hi, I’m Deema Al Zoubi, a freshman at NYU Abu Dhabi.

This website was inspired by a video my colleague Jania Jones and I filmed in just an hour for an Interactive Media class. That experience sparked the idea for Dreamscape Diaries—a space dedicated to exploring the mystery and meaning of dreams.

I’ve always been fascinated by the world of dreams and what they reveal. Here, I share insights on dreaming, provide tools to help you track and understand your own dreams, and offer resources like flashcards, journals, and a calendar to keep track of your dreams!
